Welcome!

Hello! I'm Jenny Lam-Chowdhury, a Venezuelan-Chinese-American living in New York and this site is dedicated to one of my life's dreams.

As a food lover and explorer, I don't only eat or discover new flavors, I really enjoy hearing the stories behind our food, local cuisines, and how every dish and ingredient is intertwined with the culture of the people that prepare and share them.

I believe in the power of information and how it can change lives. But I know that not everyone has the same access and many are limited by the barrier of language or are deaf or hard of hearing. I'm very committed to use my language and subtitling skills to help make this information accessible to more people around the world.

I really enjoy (good, real) food and cooking (from scratch, as much as possible) - they are essential in my daily life. But I also like to read about food or watch cooking programs, food series, documentaries and films.

I hope you learn and enjoy the content that I have personally picked and curated for my blog , and please share it with others who might also enjoy watching these videos with subtitles.
